How I become a Software Developer...

Exploring the unknown and keeping my dreams alive

I discovered my passion for programming when I was a research assistant in a robotics lab during college. I was fascinated by how these lines of codes can create something so potential.

Having zero knowledge of programming, I taught myself by all sorts of online resources and went auditing in class.

Inorder to learn more and to learn fatser, I applied for Product Operation Intern at PicColalge, a technogoloy startup for creating the best photo editing products. There I learned a great amount of product managing and I was improving everyay by learning from the best software engineers around me. By the end of the internship, I started to assist in re-designing the webpage using HTML and CSS.

Later on, I applied for a master's program of Computer Science @ NYU. With a scholarship, I came to NYU to keep pursuing my dream in 2016.

Today is hard, tomorrow is worse, but the day after tomorrow will be sunshine

Embrace challenges and keep learning everyday

After coming to New York, I learned something everyday. From the lectures at school to the overwhelming everyday life in New York.

I believed that if you want something you've never had, you must be willing to do something you've never done. Thus, I immersed myself with challenging projects from building scalable micro-services to building prediction model with big data analysis tools and machine learning methods.
